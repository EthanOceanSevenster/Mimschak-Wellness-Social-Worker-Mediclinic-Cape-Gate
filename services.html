How I Can Help

I offer a range of professional social work services to support individuals and families through various challenges. Each service is delivered with compassion, confidentiality, and a commitment to your wellbeing.

Stress Management

Learn effective coping strategies and techniques to manage stress, anxiety, and overwhelming emotions in daily life.

HIV/AIDS Support

Confidential counselling, emotional support, and guidance for individuals and families affected by HIV/AIDS.

Family Interventions

Professional mediation and support to improve family dynamics, resolve conflicts, and strengthen relationships.

Abuse Support

Safe, confidential support for survivors of domestic violence, emotional abuse, and other forms of mistreatment.

Bereavement Counselling

Compassionate support to help you navigate grief and loss, honouring your journey through mourning.

Marital & Divorce Issues

Support for couples facing relationship challenges, separation, or divorce with guidance and mediation.

Assessments

Professional social work assessments for various purposes including court reports and welfare evaluations.

Trauma Support

Specialised support for individuals dealing with traumatic experiences, PTSD, and crisis situations.

Substance Abuse

Counselling and support for individuals struggling with addiction and substance abuse issues.

Virtual Sessions

Online counselling sessions via video call for clients who prefer remote support from the comfort of home.
